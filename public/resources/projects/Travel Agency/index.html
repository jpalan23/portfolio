About Jay Tours & Travels

Jay Tours & Travels is a leading Tours & Travel website company based in Mumbai. We provide integrated travel experience to expedition enthusiasts across the world!

TRAVEL MORE. STRESS LESS

Jay Tours & Travels is a full-service travel company dedicated at providing corporate, leisure, meeting & management services. We provide integrated travel experience to expedition enthusiasts across the world! Our 8 years of experience has earned us a niche in the Travel industry.

Our company provides services in all travel related activities like Airline ticketing, Rail ticketing, Car hire, Hotel booking, Passport & VISA services. We aim at providing quality service to our valuable clients, round the clock, at very competitive rates. We have triumphantly served our clients with the most memorable experience; this has helped us built a loyal base of customers who vouch by our services. With this website we aspire to grow our reach far & beyond, and cater more and more voyagers who are looking for a travel rush in India and abroad. From beautiful beaches, to historic masterpieces, to jungles, wildlife and man-made future cities.
